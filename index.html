StuffShare

I pitched StuffShare and was voted on by my peers to lead the team on this week-long project. I was the team lead, and was responsible for project management and planning (using Trello and Slack), schema and API design, search functionality and integration with Google Maps API, and overall project vision. StuffShare is a platform for people to share and borrow items and experiences with friends and neighbors. This is a single page AngularJS app built on Rails API deployed on Heroku that also uses Google Maps API, Postgresql, Amazon AWS, and Send Grid.

Hyped

My vision for Hyped was an app that lets users easily track, rate, and share movies, music, and books. With just a few short days to build it out, I decided on an MVP that allowed users to search for movies (using the Rotten Tomatoes API) to add to their watch list, favorite movies they enjoyed, and mark movies as watched. Needs some styling work. Ruby on Sinatra app using Rotten Tomatoes api, AJAX, jQuery, and JavaScript.
